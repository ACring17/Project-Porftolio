About Andrew


I graduated from Arizona State University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Exercise Science & Health. After graduating, I flew out to Munich, Germany and brought back a new workout concept that used electric muscle stimulation for fast and effective workouts. For 3 years, I was able to grow the studio to over 100 members and achieved amazing weight loss and strength goals for my clients. After being forced to shut down due to the COVID-19 pandemic, I picked up a few books and started learning how to code with HTML, CSS, & JavaScript. I fell in love with front-end development and have been practicing every day so I can make a career change into the web development field.
